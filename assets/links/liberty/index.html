About Us

Liberty Messenger Inc. has been in the same day courier business since 1989. From our new office building in Toronto Ontario, we are ideally located to provide delivery services within the GTA. Our focus is on making on-time deliveries using courteous and professional drivers. Our success at maintaining that focus has earned us a reputation for delivering as we promised.
